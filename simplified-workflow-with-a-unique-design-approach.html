The Brief

Our client is a supply chain manager that includes inventory handling, logistics supply, and warehouse management. Evoke made a detailed analysis of the designing process and delivered the best solution.

Business Challenge

Overcome challenges in retail supply chain management, including limited visibility, communication breakdowns, and inadequate data analysis.

Solution

Streamlined retail supply chain with enhanced visibility, simplified processes, data analytics, automation, and robust security, boosting efficiency and reducing costs.

Design Process

A detailed approach was followed in the design process that helped us to deliver a solution in order to resolve the issue. In UCD, the design team includes users through research/surveys to provide a seamless experience for the product.
