Case Study

Revitalizing User Experience to Drive Engagement and Traffic on Matrimony Websites

Industry - Matrimony

RESEARCH
  • Discovery
  • Heuristic Evaluation
STRATEGY
  • Design Thinking
  • Information Architecture
DESIGN
  • User Experience
  • User Interface

The Brief

Our client wanted to re-platform their existing application and change the functionalities. They wanted to switch from a traditional scanning and searching process to a full-fledged e-commerce application and amplify user experience.

Business Challenge

Address declining website traffic by analyzing user behavior and addressing usability concerns.

Solution

Implemented a UX/UI redesign with intuitive dashboards, streamlined navigation flows, and visually appealing interfaces, resulting in increased user engagement and website traffic.

Empathise

We listed down a few questions that could give us a detailed sneak peek into their problem areas. This has helped us to identify the key areas that required additional research work.

What users prefer mostly websites or mobile app? What negates them from using the website?

Learn why this website is needed and how they can stay ahead of their competitors

Who are the users?

User demographics can help us build targeted design

Which features attract the users and which ones turn them off?

By focusing on the positive and negative aspects of a website, there are higher chances for improvement

What is the probability of the users finding a person who matches their interests?

Helping users to achieve goals improves usability and increases their number

Which one is easy to use the website or the mobile app?

The website should be easy to use. Higher usability increases user attraction

Where are the users located?

User location can help people connect culturally with the website

Discover

A complete data-driven approach followed by stakeholder mapping, competitive and consumer analysis, user personification, and a survey.

Pain Point Analysis

Out of the website’s many aspects from a user point of view, our team recognized the pain points.

Website Flow

The website navigation seems daunting

Similarity Match

Lacks the option to search for desired qualities in a partner

Membership/Payment

Membership and payment options are time-consuming

Category

The category of people who can sign up is very few

Complete Security

Concerns about security are raised because the website does not do any verification process.

Chat Option

It is difficult to find clarification’s to queries in the absence of assistance.

Design

Innovative, clean, and minimalist style that works for all.
