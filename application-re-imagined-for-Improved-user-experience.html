Enhancing User Experience for Improved Engagement and Retention in a Manufacturing App

Industry - Manufacturing

STRATEGY
  • Design Thinking
  • Information Architecture
DESIGN
  • User Experience

The Brief

Our client wanted to re-platform their existing application and change the functionalities. They wanted to switch from a traditional scanning and searching process to a full-fledged e-commerce application and amplify user experience.

Business Challenge

Enhance purchase workflow by addressing inconsistent and difficult app functionality.

Solution

Redesigned UX/UI with a minimalist design approach, improving the user experience and driving increased client conversions and sales.

Benefits

The application is capable of successfully driving 200 monthly installs and increased conversion rates due to better purchasing experience. Both apps can run smoothly, acquiring 99.9% crash-free sessions.

Approach

Our experts created an easily accessible platform to drive users worldwide.

A design process with a smooth purchasing experience enabled the users to customize the product according to their need.

A smooth onboarding process, scanning, and searching features were discovered via a thorough research.

Personalization

Multiple filters and steps used by step wizard approach for personalization and customization.

Accessibility

We delivered a clean and minimalistic style that is aesthetically appealing and engaging to the users.

Consistency

Seamless experience across mobile and desktop.

Seamless Animations

Delightful micro-interactions are introduced on a purpose to educate.

Design

Approached with clean and minimalist style, for aesthetically appealing and engaging to their users.
