Automated Verification of Top Coat Paint Values for Improved Quality and Efficiency in Manufacturing

The Brief

Our client is a leading coatings business delivering customers with innovative, colorful, and sustainable solutions. We developed the EBSA app that monitors the values recorded while coat paint is being tested. These values need to be verified by scientists to pass color-grade quality for painting

Business Challenge

Automate and optimize the manual verification process to reduce errors and improve efficiency.

Solution

Developed an automated application to streamline verification process by storing data, generating reports, and providing real-time feedback.

Discover

Different steps during the design process aided us to provide a solution-based approach to resolve challenges. In USD, the designers involve users through research and surveys for offering a robust experience of the product/service.
