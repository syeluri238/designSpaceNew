Case Study

Streamlining Artwork Creation and Approval for a Pharmaceutical Company

Industry - Pharmaceutical

RESEARCH
  • Design Thinking
  • Information Architecture
DESIGN
  • User Experience

The Brief

Our client is a leading pharmaceutical firm committed to discovering, developing. and delivering new medicines to patients with serious diseases, infections, and viruses. Evoke designed a scalable portal for business growth

Business Challenge

Create a user-friendly portal for artwork requests, approvals, inspection plans creation, and data sorting.

Solution

Developed an application with wizard patterns, Stepper functionality, and artwork request transit status tracking, enabling easy monitoring, and enhancing user experience.

Discover

A 5-step workflow was followed to make the design more user-friendly and effective. This resolved issues and kept the user at the center of analysis for User Centric Design outcomes while having a positive outcome on the business

The Outcome

Our research shows the portal needs to be designed in a scalable manner so that new modules can be easily added in the future.`

02

Segregate graphs in case of multiple data holding

03
