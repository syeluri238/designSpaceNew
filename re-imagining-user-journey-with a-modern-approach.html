The Brief

Our client is a leading pharmaceutical firm committed to discovering, developing. and delivering new medicines to patients with serious diseases, infections, and viruses. Evoke designed a scalable portal for business growth

Business Challenge

Address decreased productivity and user frustration caused by a limited and non-user-friendly legacy application.

Solution

Developed a cloud-based application with a streamlined user journey, resulting in improved user experience, increased productivity, and enhanced user satisfaction.

Design Process

We followed a 4D process to deliver a seamless user experience. The process includes different stages starting from initial research along with the user and target audience, understanding pain points, to closely observing the workplace environment. This effort created the foundation of our thought process and execution.

The Outcome

After thorough research and analysis, our team proposed a modern system aligning to the business needs and specifically designed for present and future users.
