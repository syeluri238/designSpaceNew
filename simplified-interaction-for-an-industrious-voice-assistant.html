The Brief

An industry leader in machinery design and digital innovation, our client provides a unique application with voice-enabled technology that listens to human machine operators and assists them in accurately and efficiently completing their tasks.

Business Challenge

Overcome challenges of a complex and outdated voice-enabled application, affecting learnability, productivity, customer acquisition, and retention.

Solution

Implemented a task-based mechanism with step-by-step navigation, status notifications, and enhanced design based on user analysis, reducing costs and improving sales.

Key Challenges

  • This Legacy application is designed on Window(Win2000) based design pattern.
  • Beginning and completion points are not well-defined. So user journey is completely missing.
  • Used verbiage and nomenclatures are too technical. User could not understand easily what to do.
  • Overall Application is not user-centric and lacking intuitiveness, progression and simplicity.

Happy users, happier clients!

We simplified the user journey and information architecture. This effort turned the risks and costs of a negative user experience into opportunities and benefits.
