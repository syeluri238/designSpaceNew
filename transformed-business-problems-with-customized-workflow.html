The Brief

Pegasus, a part of the CSC application was built on the Pega Design System to automate and fast-forward entity filing of products.

Business Challenge

Address tax filing and order fulfillment challenges by implementing a customized dashboard and streamlined processes.

Solution

Optimized tax filing and order fulfillment with a customized Pega Design System dashboard, improving efficiency and accuracy.

Design System Usage

The goal of the design system is to create a cohesive and intuitive UX. But not all design systems work in the same manner. The Constellation Design System works differently and embraces prescribed designs for designers, business architects, and other non-technical app builders.
